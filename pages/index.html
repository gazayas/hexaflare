Controls

arrow keys: move left/right
i or k: move left/right
z or space: drop the star cluster
x or c: rotate clockwise/counter-clockwise

Controls (tip)

Use s and d (or the bumpers on a controller) to move the star cluster along the flare star one block at a time for more precise movements.

flare star

The main playing field. It has a strong gravitational pull, so all star clusters dropped into it will gravitate towards the center (the core).

star clusters

Blocks the players drops consisting of four hexagons a piece.

flare

Occurs each time star clusters develop a ring inside the flare star. Star clusters that gather in the form of a ring release energy, resulting in a flare. The flared stars will dissipate, and the remaining stars will gravitate towards the center.

Corona

The outer portion of the flare star on which star clusters move along. The game will finish if you drop a star cluster and it lands outside of the main flare star's field of gravity and into the corona,
